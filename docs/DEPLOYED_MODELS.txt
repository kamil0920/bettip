LEGEND:
  Wave = W14 or W13 or W12 or W11A/B/C or W10B or W9A/B/C or W8A/8B or W6B or W5
  Prec = Holdout precision (primary ranking metric)
  ECE  = Expected Calibration Error (<0.10 required)
  FVA  = Forecast Value Added vs market baseline (higher = more skill)
  #F   = Number of features used by model
  Tau  = Conformal tau (overconfidence correction)
  Odds = REAL (bookmaker) or EST (Poisson-estimated, ROI unreliable)
  20?  = Passes min_bets=20 gate (Y/N)

NOTE: ROI omitted for EST-odds markets (inflated by circular Poisson loop).
      For REAL-odds markets, ROI is shown but sample sizes are small — trust precision + FVA more.
